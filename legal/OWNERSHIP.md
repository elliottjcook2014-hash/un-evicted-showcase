### 1. Core Ownership
All rights to the game "Un-evicted," including the brand name "Turquoise Cow Games," the game design, the story, and the underlying source code, are owned 100% by the Founder (Elliott).

### 2. Music and Audio
All musical compositions, loops, and sound effects produced for this project are the sole property of Elliott. 
* Team members are granted a temporary license to listen to these files for development purposes only.
* Distribution or use of these tracks outside of this project is strictly prohibited.

### 3. Contributed Assets (Sprites and Art)
By pushing art assets (pixel art, UI, textures) to this repository, the contributor agrees to the following:
* **Exclusive License:** The art is provided to Turquoise Cow Games for use in "Un-evicted" and all future versions of the game.
* **No Revocation:** The contributor cannot "take back" the art or demand its removal once it has been integrated into the project.
* **Portfolio Use:** Contributors may show their work in a personal portfolio only with a "Watermark" or written credit to Turquoise Cow Games.

### 4. Derivative Works
Members may not create "fan games," sequels, or mods using project assets without the express written permission of the Lead Dev.

# Studio Code of Conduct & Work Ethic
**Studio:** Turquoise Cow Games  
**Lead Developer:** Elliott  

---

### 1. Work Hours
To maintain our standards, the Lead Developer commits to **3 hours of daily progress**. 
* **Contributors:** While contributors may have different schedules, they are expected to provide weekly updates on their assigned assets.
* **Communication:** If a member cannot work, they must notify the Lead Developer in advance via Session or GitHub.

### 2. Professionalism
* **No "Dead Weight":** Members who do not contribute to the repository for more than 14 days without notice may be moved to "Inactive" status or removed from the Organization.
* **No Toxicity:** Respect the vision of the project. Disputes over game design are settled by the Lead Developer (Elliott).
* **Firing Policy:** The Lead Developer reserves the right to terminate a member's access if they hinder the project's progress or violate the NCA/Ownership policies.

### 3. Quality Standards
We do not settle for "Standard" quality. 
* All music must be high-fidelity.
* All pixel art must fit the established "Pixel-Art" style.
* All code must be commented and pushed to the correct branch.

**Acknowledgment:** By remaining a member of this GitHub Organization, you agree to uphold these standards.
